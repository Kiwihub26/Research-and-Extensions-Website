About the Team

Get To Know The People Behind Research & Extension.

The Office of Research and Extensions is powered by a dynamic team, each member contributing a distinct set of skills and passion to our collective mission. At the helm is a visionary leader with decades of experience, guiding our efforts towards agricultural innovation. A renowned botanist heads our horticulture research, while a dedicated entomologist spearheads our pest management endeavors, ensuring sustainable solutions. A talented community outreach manager nurtures vital connections between our research and local farmers. Rounding out the team, a skilled data scientist employs advanced analytics to drive informed decision-making. United, this team embodies our commitment to advancing agricultural knowledge and empowering the community.
