Conducting Research & Extension in New Normal: A FOCUS ON GENDER-SENSITIVE APPROACHES

June 03, 2022

The Cavite State University – CCAT Campus recognizes the essentiality of research-productive and extension-sensitive faculty members. Hence, this proposed webinar by the Gender and Development Unit and Research and Extension Office focused on equipping and developing Gender-Sensitive Researchers and Extensionists.

We invite the Cavite State University – CCAT Campus Researchers and Extensionists and the other interested participants to this Webinar entitled: “Conducting Research and Extension in New Normal: A Focus on Gender-Sensitive Approaches” which will be held on June 3, 2022, From 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM via Cisco WebEx and Facebook Live.
